Our Story — Made with Heart

HanName began with a simple belief: a name is a bridge. For friends around the world, a Chinese name is not a translation — it is a welcoming doorway into a living culture.

Each name we craft balances sound (how it feels to say), meaning (virtues you value), and heritage (classical references from texts like Classic of Poetry and Chu Ci). We also offer handwritten blessings by respected seniors — turning every name into a wish passed from one generation to the next.

Keep it as a digital certificate, or seal it in a traditional stamp. Either way, it’s a keepsake of your story in Chinese characters.
